Hockey Support Information

General Information

Hockey is a fast-paced sport played on ice, where two teams compete to score goals by hitting a puck into the opponent's net using sticks.

Rules of the Game

  • Each team has six players on the ice: three forwards, two defensemen, and one goaltender.
  • The game is played in three periods of 20 minutes each.
  • Players can be penalized for infractions such as tripping, high-sticking, and fighting.

Equipment

Essential hockey equipment includes:

  • Skates
  • Stick
  • Helmet
  • Protective gear (shoulder pads, elbow pads, shin guards, gloves)
